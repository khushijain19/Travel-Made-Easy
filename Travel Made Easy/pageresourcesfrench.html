RESOURCES FRENCH

On this page you can find a lot of resources that will help you getting a first impression of a country. Music provides good access to the language and culture of a country. The music videos below can facilitate your learning process. Not only learning vocabulary but having a context, melody and certain pictures in front of you can help you remembering newly learnt expressions. What we also recommend is to watch movies or series in the new language with English subtitles. In that way you have a great unconscious learning effect!
