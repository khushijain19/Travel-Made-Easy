ABOUT US

This page was created for the Code First Girls competition by Khushi, Janina, and Annais.

As three young students in London, with various different cultural backgrounds, and separate linguistic talents developed over our lives, we have sought to compile a straightforward guide to language to help teach each other and others the must-know basics of a language in order to travel easily and confidently in another country!

With guides to essential phrases in French, German, and Spanish, combined with resources for further language exploration, and tips to really grasp a new language, our goal is to help others experience other countries the way we can, with less stress in communicating!
